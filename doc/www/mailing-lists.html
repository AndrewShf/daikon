Daikon mailing lists

If you use Daikon, please sign up for one or both of the following mailing lists:
  • daikon-announce: low-volume, announcements of new releases
  • daikon-discuss: moderated, forum for users
You can also send mail to daikon-developers@pag.lcs.mit.edu, to reach the developers actively working on the Daikon tool. (You can also become a member of this group, if you contribute to the Daikon codebase.)
